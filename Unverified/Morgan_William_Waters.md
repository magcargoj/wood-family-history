## Biography

**Note: This record is currently under investigation for historical accuracy.**

Morgan William Waters is traditionally identified as a son of Colonel [[William_Waters_Sr]] of Virginia. However, he is not mentioned in the Colonel's 1689 will, which lists six other sons. 

Furthermore, many traditional trees list his birthplace as Bulloch County, Georgia, in 1660. This is historically anachronistic, as the Georgia colony was not founded until 1733 and Bulloch County was not formed until 1796. It is more likely that if this individual existed, he was born in Virginia or North Carolina and later generations migrated to Georgia.
