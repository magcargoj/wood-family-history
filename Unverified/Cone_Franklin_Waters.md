## Biography

**Note: This record is currently under investigation for historical accuracy.**

Cone Franklin Waters is traditionally listed as a patriarch of the Georgia Waters family. However, his birthplace is often given as Bulloch County, Georgia, in 1682, which is anachronistic by over a century.

There is also a major discrepancy regarding his wife **Lucretia Harris Bromfield**, who is listed in some records with a birth date of **1765**. This 83-year gap between husband and wife suggests a significant misattribution or conflation of multiple generations.
