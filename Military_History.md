# Service & Sacrifice: Military History

From the struggle for American independence to the industrial era, our family has a long and storied history of military service.

## 🇺🇸 The Revolutionary War
Several ancestors fought for the birth of the United States, primarily in the North Carolina regiments.
*   **[[William_Wood_1758]] (1758–1844):** A documented veteran of the Revolutionary War.
*   **[[Hardin_Warner_Sr]] (1755–1829):** Served in the 5th North Carolina Regiment.
*   **[[Willis_AKA_Wills_Wilson_Sr]] (1752–1833):** A private in the North Carolina line.

## ⚔️ The Civil War
The 1860s saw our family members serving in critical roles, often at a high personal cost.
*   **[[John_Charles_Teal]] (1838–1918):** Served in Company K, 26th North Carolina Infantry.
*   **[[Calvin_D_Brooks]] (1832–1862):** Served during the war and passed away in Tennessee in 1862.
*   **[[William_B_Joe_Moye]] (1825–1865):** A casualty of the war, passing away in Louisiana in 1865.

## 🛡️ Colonial Militia
In the early 1600s, family members were active in the protection of the early colonies.
*   **[[William_Waters_Sr]]**: Held the rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the Virginia Militia.
