# Colonial Virginia Pioneers

Our family was among the earliest English settlers in North America, establishing roots on the **Eastern Shore** and the **Tidewater** regions of Virginia in the early 1600s.

## 🏛️ Political & Social Leadership
The **Waters** and **Robins** families were prominent in the political life of early Virginia. These ancestors served as surgeons, High Sheriffs, and members of the **House of Burgesses**, helping to shape the laws and structure of the burgeoning colony.

## 🚢 The Sea Venture Connection
Our ancestor **Edward Waters** arrived in Virginia under extraordinary circumstances. He was a survivor of the *Sea Venture* shipwreck in Bermuda in 1609, an event famously believed to have inspired Shakespeare's *The Tempest*.
