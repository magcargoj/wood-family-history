# Welcome to the Family Archive

This project documents the merged histories of the **Wood** and **Brooks** family lines. Our research spans continents—from the coal mines of **Slovakia** to the early settlements of **Colonial Virginia** and **Connecticut**.

## 🌳 Explore the Tree
The heart of our research is the **[[Family_Tree|Complete Family Tree]]**. 
*   **Jeremy's Line:** Tracing the Wood and Arnold families through North Carolina.
*   **Ashley's Line:** Exploring the Brooks, Almond, and Warner lineages.
*   **Our Roots:** Connecting back to the 1500s in Wales and the 1600s in Germany.

## 📜 Key Storylines
Discover the major chapters of our family history:
*   **[[Slovakia|The Slovakian Migration]]:** The journey of the Kruppa and Szeles families from Debraď and Stankovany.
*   **[[Virginia|Colonial Virginia]]:** Early pioneers in the Virginia House of Burgesses and the struggle of the early 1600s.
*   **[[North_Carolina|North Carolina Pioneers]]:** Generations of farmers, soldiers, and families building NC.
*   **[[Military_History|Service & Sacrifice]]:** From the Revolutionary War to the Civil War and beyond.
