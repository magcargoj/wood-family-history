# North Carolina Pioneers

North Carolina is the central hub of our family history, with generations of the **Wood**, **Brooks**, **Almond**, and **Wilson** families settling, farming, and building communities across the state.

## 🚜 The Paternal Heartlands
The **Wood** family has deep roots in **Harnett**, **Johnston**, and **Wake** counties. From the late 1700s to today, this line has been a constant presence in the North Carolina landscape.

## 🏔️ The Mountain Branches
The **Brooks** and **Matney** families established themselves in the rugged terrain of **Ashe** and **Avery** counties, living a life of self-sufficiency in the Blue Ridge Mountains.
