## Biography

Oscar Rae Waters was born on August 7, 1890, in Dukes, Bradford County, Florida. He was the son of **James Que Waters** and **Martha "Mattie" Louise Thomas**. This is supported by the 1885 marriage of James Que and Mattie in Georgia and their subsequent move to Florida.

In the 1900 US Census, an Oscar R. Waters is listed in the household of William W. Waters and Eliza Anderson. While listed as "Son," it is likely he was living with his great-uncle William W. (James Que's uncle) at the time, or the relationship was misrecorded. The specific birth date and parentage in the family records (James Que & Mattie) are considered the primary identification.

He worked as a telegraph operator for the railroad. He married Iva B. Lightsey in 1909 and later Essie Rae Malphurs. He passed away on December 19, 1965, in Brooker, Bradford County, Florida, and is buried in Newmansville Cemetery.
