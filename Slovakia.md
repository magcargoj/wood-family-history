# The Slovakian Migration

Our family’s roots in Central Europe run deep, specifically in the regions of **Stankovany** and **Debraď** in what is now modern-day Slovakia.

## ⚒️ The Industrial Journey
The **Kruppa** and **Szeles** families were part of the late 19th-century wave of migration from the Austro-Hungarian Empire to the industrial heartlands of Pennsylvania. Many ancestors worked in the coal mines and steel mills that powered the American industrial revolution.

## 📍 Key Ancestral Villages
*   **Stankovany:** Home to the **Kruppa**, **Matko**, and **Spireng** lines for generations.
*   **Debraď:** The ancestral seat of the **Szeles** and **Benyitzki** families.
