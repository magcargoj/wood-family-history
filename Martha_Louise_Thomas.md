## Biography

Martha Louise "Mattie" Thomas was born in 1864. She married **James Que Waters** on Christmas Eve, 1885, in Bulloch County, Georgia. Shortly after their marriage, the family moved to Florida, where their children were born. She passed away in Winter Garden, Florida, in 1939.
