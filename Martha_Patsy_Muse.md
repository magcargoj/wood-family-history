# Martha Patsy Muse

**Note: This individual has been identified as [[Lydia_P_Muse]].**

Earlier records identified the mother of [[Jefferson_Davis_Medlin]] as Martha Patsy Muse. Further research and census records have confirmed her name as Lydia P. Muse (1822-1904). Please see the [[Lydia_P_Muse]] note for accurate details.
